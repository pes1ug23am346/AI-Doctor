Asthma is a chronic respiratory disease that causes inflammation and narrowing of the airways.

Common Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Shortness of breath
- Chest tightness
- Coughing, especially at night

Triggers:
- Dust
- Pollen
- Smoke
- Cold air
- Respiratory infections
- Exercise

Risk Factors:
- Family history
- Allergies
- Air pollution
- Smoking

Complications:
- Frequent breathing difficulties
- Reduced quality of life
- Emergency hospital visits

Treatment:
- Inhalers
- Bronchodilators
- Corticosteroids
- Avoiding triggers

Prevention:
- Avoid allergens
- Follow prescribed treatment
- Regular medical checkups
