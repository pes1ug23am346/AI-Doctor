Hypertension, also known as high blood pressure, is a condition in which the force of blood against artery walls remains consistently high.

Normal Blood Pressure:
- Around 120/80 mmHg

Common Symptoms:
- Often has no symptoms
- Headaches
- Dizziness
- Shortness of breath
- Chest pain (in severe cases)

Risk Factors:
- Obesity
- High salt intake
- Smoking
- Excess alcohol consumption
- Family history
- Stress
- Lack of physical activity

Complications:
- Heart attack
- Stroke
- Kidney disease
- Heart failure
- Vision problems

Treatment:
- Reduce salt intake
- Regular exercise
- Weight management
- Blood pressure medications
- Stress management

Prevention:
- Healthy diet
- Regular physical activity
- Avoid smoking
- Limit alcohol intake
- Regular blood pressure monitoring
